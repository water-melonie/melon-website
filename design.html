Chompers the Chair: Original Sketches


(March 2022)
(18in x 12.5in)
(Pencil, fine liners, and color pencils)
For a project in my Interior Design class, I had to design a chair.
Originally, me and a friend wanted to design an Alice in
Wonderland themed furniture set, but my design for a Cheshire
cat themed chair eventually turned into this: A giant cat chair
that eats you when you sit in it and a cozy little hide-away
for when life gets too overwhelming.

Chompers the Chair: Technical Drawing


(October 2022)
(Adobe Illustrator)
For my Advanced Digital Media class, I had
to make an vector illustration in Adobe Illustrator based
off a piece of artwork that I had made before. I decided
that Chompers would be the perfect subject matter. I created
technical drawings to show Chompers' different components,
their functions, and how they were attached together.

Chompers the Chair: Model


(March 2022 - May 2022)
(5.5in x 6.5in x 4.5in)
(Cotton canvas fabric, string, Bristol paper, etc.)
This is a continuation of the designing a chair project.
Making a physical model was optional, but I really wanted
to make Chompers come to life. I made a Bristol paper frame,
glued fabric on to that frame, and then hand sewed and
stuffed the different limbs before hot gluing them on to
the frame. The cover of the chair model is functional
with the ability to open and close, creating a enclosed
space for a person to crawl inside. Chompers' arm rests are
literally the cat's arms and paws which can hold beverages
or snacks for you. I photoshopped myself into the chair to
show how it would look if someone actually sat in it.
