PROJECT #5
Both of Us (2022)
Graphic Novel - Procreate

For the final project of a Graphic Novel class, everyone created their own short story graphic novels. My graphic novel was inspired by a conversation I had with a childhood friend on our way to our high schools. She said, “At least one of us gets to do what we want.” I've known this friend for about 9 years now and throughout these 9 years both of us have changed immensely. I've decided to pursue my passions while she has chosen to fulfill expectations. Neither path is easy. The two characters in the graphic novel are based on the two of us. The graphic novel plays with symbolism using flowers and juxtaposition between reality and fantasy as they grow older.
