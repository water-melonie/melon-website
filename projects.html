PROJECT #1
Hatsune Miku World Tour Concert Flyer (2026)
Principles of Visual Design Project - Adobe Illustrator, Procreate

A digital flyer for the virtual vocaloid singer Hatsune Miku and her imaginary upcoming starlight themed world tour. I conveyed the vocaloid/J-Pop and pop music genre through whimsical and bold typography. I modified the type in Adobe Illustrator to include a star for the letter 'A' and used my illustration of Miku for the letter 'I'. I also recreated Miku's logo/name in Japanese with the same bold type style as the rest of the text and incorporated it in the background of the design to fill up space and add visual interest.
