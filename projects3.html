PROJECT #4
Chompers the Depression Muncher (2022)
Interior/Industrial Design - Mixed Media, Adobe Illustrator

For a project in an Interior Design class, I had to design a chair. Inspired by Alice in Wonderland and the Cheshire cat, I designed a giant cat chair that eats you when you sit in it to become a cozy little hide-away for when life gets too overwhelming. Making a physical model was optional, but I really wanted to make Chompers come to life. I made a Bristol paper frame, glued fabric on to that frame, and then hand sewed and stuffed the different limbs before hot gluing them on to the frame. The cover of the chair model is functional with the ability to open and close, creating a enclosed space for a person to crawl inside. Chompers' arm rests are literally the cat's arms and paws which can hold beverages or snacks for you. I photoshopped myself into the chair to show how it would look if someone actually sat in it. Although the chair would be impractically big if it were built to fit a grown adult, it could serve as a fun chair for children. But I think adults would enjoy sitting in Chompers too, even if they can't fit it in their own home. Finally, I made technical drawings in Adobe Illustrator to show Chompers' different components, their functions, and how they were attached together.
