PROJECT #3
Fitness App (2026)
Principles of Visual Design Project - Figma

This fitness app is made for people like me who don't like to workout and need extra motivation. One of the main reasons I don't like to workout is that I don't know what exercises to do and I don't want to spend time planning a workout. I designed this app to put all the exercises in a list of tasks that you can easily accomplish and check off. It has premade workouts so you can easily pick and choose what you want to do. I also really like idea of Tamagotchi virtual or digital pets that motivate you to keep going, so I designed a cat mascot/virtual pet that gets more and more buff as you work out.
