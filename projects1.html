PROJECT #2
With Hue (2025)
Media Device Architecture Final Project - Game Boy Advance Game

A story based game in a Madoka Magica inspired universe where you play as a normal girl at day and magical girl by night. Every night you patrol your town looking for people suffering from their negative emotions. As a magical girl you can see people’s emotions and each of their emotions are represented by a color. When negative emotions overwhelm someone, they are surrounded by violent and angry colored auras that lash out and affect the colors of people around them. In order to save them, you have to match your colors to theirs and slowly desaturate their colors in a I love hue color puzzle mechanic.
